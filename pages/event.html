EVENTS AT SCHOOL

Teachers' Day

This day is celebrated all over the planet on Gurupurnima, showing respect and reverence for the Guru. On teachers' day, every year senior students organise the program and junior ones participate in different joyful activities like dancing, singing, reciting poems, cracking jokes, etc. school premises. Teachers together cut the cake brought by seniors during the celebration. In addition to these, each class also celebrates the day with their respective class teachers before the main event.

Tour

The tour is the place where the group of people visit different destination .In my school our Senior co-odinator organized the tour for class 10 and 9 .The tours lasted for 4 days 3 nights in pokhara,palpa and lumbini which the the most popular places in nepal. our school reserved proper bus and hotel according to the students requirement. Others 4 teachers also visit tour with stuents for mentoring them.

Environment day

The theme for World Environment Day on 5 June 2023 focussed on solutions to plastic pollution under the campaign #BeatPlasticPollution. The world is being inundated by plastic.so our school also put some efforts to reduce plastics and planting more tree and plants. On that day all teachers wars green dress to celebrate the Environment day .Students also plant's tree and herds to make school environment clean and peace.

Yamari Punhi

Yamari Punhi is a Newari festival marking the end of the rice harvest. A yamari is a confection of rice flour (from the new harvest) dough shaped like fish and filled with brown cane sugar and sesame seeds, which is then steamed.In our school our school care takers and teachers made yomari and distribute to all the students and teachers.

Visit of professors from Chungbuk National University

The professors and the student of Chungbuk National University visited our school to learn about nepal's school environment.And the culture and education system of nepal's school. They also thought the students about their culture too. They also thought students and teachers to learn and memorized in a fun way. They saw the small projects made by the students of steam club and compliment them too. Our school welcomed and farewell them respectly.

Childrens day

Children's Day is a commemorative date celebrated annually in honor of children, whose date of observance varies by country. In nepal it is celebrate in the month of bhadra(september).In our school teachers made present for the students by themselves.There happen to be program where the prize won by students in whole years by participating and winnig in different competition.

Space Robotics Session

One day session of robotics was organized by naso.where the student of our school also participated. From that session our students a lot things about robotics and Space. They also try to make small robotics model.
