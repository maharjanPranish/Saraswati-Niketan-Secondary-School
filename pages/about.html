What Principal/Ms. Maharjan says...

With more than 7 decades of glorious history. Saraswati Niketan Secondary School still stands with its head even higher. Established in 2004 B.S., it has produced quite a big number of graduates who are serving the nation in one and other ways. In the changing scenario Saraswati Niketan Secondary School is trying to inculcate the 21 century skills in students in order to enable them to compete globally. Today, we are offering Computer Engineering courses of school level technical stream program from grade 9 to 12 funded by Kathmandu Metropolitan City. With qualified teaching faculties and well equipped laboratories, it is providing ample opportunities to make them competent and technically skilled to flourish in the future job market.

Visions of school

To be a model school that provides holistic education.
To produce intellectually sound and socially responsible citizens who can compete in the global market.
Creating a holistic learning enviroment where all learners regardless of their background, race and ethinicity that can foster their inherent capabilities.
